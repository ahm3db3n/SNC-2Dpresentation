Environmental Issues

Let's learn about how chemistry affects our planet!

Oil Spills

  • Ways to Clean Up:
  • 1. Booms and Skimmers - Helps collect oil from water
  • 2. Dispersants - Break oil into tiny pieces
  • 3. Bioremediation - Uses bacteria to eat the oil

Car Exhaust Pollution

  • Formula: 2C₈H₁₈ + 25O₂ → 16CO₂ + 18H₂O
  • Bad Effects: Causes smog and makes it hard to breathe
  • What Helps? Catalytic converters clean the air before it comes out of the car

Gold Mining with Cyanide

  • How It Works:
  • 1. Cyanide dissolves gold
  • 2. Zinc helps get gold back
  • 3. Gold is purified
  • Problem: Cyanide is toxic to animals and water

How Pools Stay Clean

  • What Makes Pools Dirty? Sweat, pee, bacteria
  • How to Clean: Chlorine kills germs
  • Chemistry: Cl₂ + H₂O → HCl + HOCl (Hypochlorous acid kills bacteria)

Ammonia & Bleach - A Bad Mix!

  • Why is it Dangerous? It makes toxic gas
  • Formula: NH₃ + NaOCl → NH₂Cl + NaOH

What We Learned

These chemical reactions affect our environment. We need to be careful how we use them!
